# Email Marketing Campaign Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
A multi-page Power BI dashboard analyzing email marketing campaign performance — covering engagement rates, device/domain impact, geographic reach, and conversion tracking across campaign types.

## 🎯 Problem Statement
Understand which campaign types, devices, and regions drive the strongest engagement and conversions, and identify where bounce/unsubscribe rates signal list health issues.

## 🗂️ Data Source
 provided during Course completion period at Rows & Columns (email campaign data, July 2024– september 2024)

## 🛠️ Tools Used
Power BI, DAX, Power Query

## 🔍 Key Analysis
**Page 1 — Campaign Performance Overview**
- Device-type impact on campaign performance (tablet, desktop, mobile)
- Campaign performance by country (geographic heat map)
- Bounce rate by email domain (Gmail, Yahoo, Outlook, AOL, Hotmail)
- Top 10 campaigns ranked by click rate

**Page 2 — Conversion & Engagement Trends**
- Click rate trends by month, segmented by conversion target (download, form completion, purchase, sign-up, web visit)
- Opened-rate by client segment (customer vs lead) across countries
- Country-level conversion rates for monthly campaigns
- Mail distribution by device type

## 💡 Key Insights
- Out of 92K total emails sent, click rate stood at 2.18%, with bounce and unsubscribe rates kept low at 0.03% and 0.05%
- "Discount offer for active influencers" was the top-performing campaign with a 14.73% click rate
- Tablet devices drove the highest engagement at 46.06K interactions, ahead of desktop and mobile combined
- AOL and Yahoo accounted for the largest share of bounces among email domains (29.46% and 27.91%)
- Conversion target "Download" peaked in August at 9.71% click rate, the strongest-performing target across the campaign period
- Iceland led country-level conversion rates for July campaigns at 100%, though Bulgaria and Lithuania showed stronger conversion volume at scale
